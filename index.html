I'm Salman, a mechanical engineering student at Aditya University with a strong curiosity for building and exploring ideas beyond my core field. I'm not just focused on academics — I like experimenting with technology, especially web development, automation, and AI-based tools.

"I don't wait to feel ready. I start, break things, fix them, and ship anyway. That's how I've learned everything worth knowing."

I spend time learning how systems work, whether it's coding small tools, creating websites, or trying to automate repetitive tasks. I'm particularly interested in combining creativity with technology — building things that are not only functional but also visually engaging. If it's interesting, I'll try to build it.

I enjoy working on personal projects like portfolio sites, affiliate content systems, and experimenting with platforms like Pinterest and GitHub. I've also explored running code for AI assistants and learning how different tools integrate together — there's something fascinating about making disconnected systems talk to each other.

"My goal is to become someone who can turn ideas into real digital products — and eventually create systems that generate value at scale."

Right now I'm in that phase where everything feels like a learning curve, and honestly? I like it that way. Every project teaches me something the internet doesn't. Every failure narrows the gap between where I am and where I want to be.

This portfolio is not about showing perfection — it's about showing progress, curiosity, and the quiet confidence that comes from actually building things, not just talking about them.

"Space doesn't wait for anyone to be ready. Neither do I."
